The Best Movies According to Eric

My top 3 movies of all-time

Interstellar

I like this movie because I like science and this movie makes me curious about the universe with all its theory

Forrest Gump

I like this movie because at first it seems like a very light-hearted film, but the more you watch and observing the details, you start to see the tragedy

One Piece

Even though this is not a movie, the story that is being written through out the anime is very intriguing that touches on many social problems of the world
